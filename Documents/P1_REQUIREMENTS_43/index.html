P1 Project requirements

Erik, Johannes, Lasse, Morten og Sebastian
21.09.2023

Our client

Mamma Mia Pizzeria is an up-and-coming pizza restaurant in the heart of Trondheim.

Purpose and goals

Our client is a new restaurant in Trondheim in need of a website to reach out to customers. Their aim is to provide high quality food in a relaxing and inviting atmosphere, and establish themselves as the best pizzeria in Trondheim. Our client hopes to gain traffic to their website by offering unique promotions and deals in the “start-up” phase. These have to be easy accessible from the home page. By offering table reservations on their website, our client wants to make it easy for their customers to plan their pizza nights in advance.

Audience

The website’s main audience will be the residents of Trondheim and the tourists who find themselves looking for a place to eat. The restaurant wants to attract people who appreciate high-quality cuisine, and it is therefore essential to provide a detailed and easily accessible menu.

Content of the Website and Organisation

  1. Home
    • This page will welcome the users to the restaurant's website.
  2. Menu
    • This page will show the different pizzas the pizzeria offers with price, also the drinking menu and any other food items. The menu should display the allergens of each pizza (in a logical and intuitive way) to make life easier for customers with allergies.
  3. Book Table
    • The user will be able to make reservations in advance, deciding on how many guests will attend as well as a date and time for the reservation.
  4. About Us
    • This page will give the user information about the pizzeria, its history, what their ambitions are etc.
  5. Contact
    • This page will contain information about how to contact (phone, email, etc.) the pizzeria for large reservations or if the customers have any questions, or if someone is looking for a job. It will also include where the restaurant is located (maybe an interactive map) and its opening hours.

Requirements first draft:

Functional:

  • The website shall include a “menu” page.
  • The website shall include a "Locations" page with addresses, and operating hours
  • The website shall include a “booking” page, where users can book a table at the restaurant.
  • The website shall include a “about us” page.
  • users should be able to access details about ongoing promotions with a single click from home page.

Non-functional:

  • Every page must load in less than 3 seconds to ensure a smooth user experience.
  • The website shall be fully responsive, adapting to various screen sizes, including smartphones and tablets.
  • Users should be able to reach any reference page in three clicks or less for ease of navigation.

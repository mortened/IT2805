About us



Our Story

Welcome to our Mamma Mia pizzeria, where Italy's pizza-making tradition is our inspiration and passion. Our roots in Italy run deep, and we've brought that heritage to the heart of Trondheim. As a family-run establishment, we take immense pride in crafting each pizza with care and the finest, locally sourced ingredients. We believe that every pizza should tell a story, and with each creation, we aim to take you on a journey through the flavors of Italy. Whether you savor a classic Margherita or an innovative creation, we're here to satisfy your pizza cravings. Join us in our warm and inviting atmosphere, and let us share our love for pizza with you. Buon appetito!



Our Mission

We long to import and create the most authentic Italian restaurant experience to the people of Trondheim. Through our many years of experience making the ever-popular Italian cuisine, we have developed and carefully selected our menu to bring the Italian specialities to Trondheim.
